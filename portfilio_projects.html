Shopify API Modernization: Migrating to GraphQL for Smarter Commerce

I led the transition from legacy Shopify REST APIs to the modern GraphQL API framework, streamlining how data is queried and delivered across storefront, order, and fulfillment systems. This upgrade reduced redundant calls, optimized payload efficiency, and improved response times by enabling clients to retrieve only the data they actually need.

What does this mean for Business?

From a business standpoint, this transformation means faster page loads, smoother checkout experiences, and lower operational costs tied to API overuse and server lag. By adopting GraphQL, I positioned the platform for scalability—supporting advanced analytics, personalized customer journeys, and real-time inventory visibility that directly enhance conversion rates, customer satisfaction, and overall revenue performance.

Operating Model Revamp: Implementing a Target Operating Model (TOM) for Scalable Growth

I redesigned the company’s operating model using a Target Operating Model (TOM) framework to align people, processes, technology, and governance with the organization’s long-term strategy. This involved mapping current-state inefficiencies, defining future-state capabilities, and implementing streamlined workflows that improved accountability and decision-making across teams. The TOM provided a clear blueprint for how the business should function end-to-end—clarifying roles, optimizing resource allocation, and ensuring technology investments directly supported strategic goals.

Is your Operating Model Costing you money?

For businesses, a well-structured TOM is critical because it transforms fragmented operations into a unified, efficient system—reducing costs, improving agility, and enabling sustainable growth in rapidly changing markets. Intentional TOM saves money because it replaces reactive operations with predictable, scalable, and measurable systems. In financial terms, mature organizations implementing TOMs often see 10–30% reductions in operating costs within the first year, along with improved productivity and strategic focus.

LTL Transportation Integration: Optimizing Freight Operations with Oracle Transportation Management (OTM)

I led the integration of LTL carrier networks into Oracle Transportation Management (OTM), enabling real-time visibility, automated rating, and streamlined shipment execution across multiple distribution centers. This project replaced manual scheduling and disconnected carrier portals with a unified logistics platform—reducing tendering time, improving on-time delivery rates, and cutting freight costs through better mode and carrier optimization. By connecting APIs between OTM, warehouse systems, and carrier EDI feeds, I helped the business gain end-to-end shipment intelligence—tracking costs, service levels, and exceptions from a single dashboard. For companies managing complex freight volumes, this level of integration matters because it translates directly into lower transportation spend, fewer service failures, and stronger customer satisfaction through predictable, data-driven delivery performance.
